Club Olimpia is another popular club in Paraguay.

-Founded on July 25, 1902, in Asunción, Paraguay.

-Titles won: 46 titles in the First Division; 3 time Champions of America from Copa Libertadores

-President: Miguel Cardona

-Coach: Francisco Javier "Chiqui" Arce Rolón
